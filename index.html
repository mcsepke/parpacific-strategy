The EC2 Snapshot Problem
Native AWS snapshots carry no compression, no encryption, and no immutability guarantees. They typically cost ~40% more than RCV alternatives, and require manually-maintained tiering policies that reduce costs slowly but never eliminate snapshot spend entirely.
🛡️
RCV as Snapshot Replacement
Rubrik Cloud Vault can replace EC2 snapshots with compressed, encrypted, immutable backups managed under a single SLA policy — eliminating snapshot costs, simplifying operations, and hardening the EC2 recovery posture against ransomware.

Rubrik Identity Recovery or Resilience directly addresses this gap.
