// 04 — Identity Protection · Active Directory & Entra ID

Identity Recovery
& Resilience

Par Pacific's existing M365, Data Center, and Cloud licenses cover basic AD and Entra ID user/group protection — but advanced operational recovery, cyber recovery, and cyber resilience capabilities require dedicated Identity SKUs.

🔑
Identity is the #1 Ransomware Attack Vector AD compromise is the most common lateral movement path in enterprise ransomware. Par Pacific has no orchestrated forest recovery capability today. A single forest outage could halt all on-prem operations.

Rubrik Editions — Identity Capabilities by License

Identity capabilities are progressively unlocked across operational recovery, cyber recovery, and cyber resilience tiers. Par Pacific's existing licenses cover only the operational baseline.
